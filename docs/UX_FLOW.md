## 60–75 second demo runbook

### Screen 1 — THE BEFORE (10s, silent)
Pixel-faithful Lofty dashboard today. 9 widgets, 47% setup nag, 6 unlabeled rail icons. Red overlay bottom-left: *"8–12 clicks before value."* Copy top-left (small): *"Every morning, 128,000 agents open this."* Hold silent for 10 seconds.

### Screen 2 — THE QUESTION (4s)
Cut to black. Two lines fade in sequentially:
1. *"Lofty has every feature a real estate agent could want."*
2. *"So why does every morning still feel like that?"*

### Screen 3 — THE ORB EMERGES (4s)
Dark navy. One line: *"What if the most powerful CRM in real estate was simpler than a conversation?"* A glowing orb fades in below, breathing, blue-to-cyan gradient.

### Screen 4 — THE MORNING BRIEFING (30–35s) — money shot
- Dark navy background, mostly empty.
- Orb (180px) at center, pulsing while speaking.
- Label above: `LOFTY AI`. Subtitle: *"Friday, April 18 · Good morning, Baylee"*.
- Voice (ElevenLabs female, warm): *"Good morning Baylee. I went through your leads, your transactions, your Smart Plans, and your inbox. Three things matter today."*
- Three action cards slide up one at a time, synced to the voice:
  - **Card 1 🔥 HOT LEAD** — Scott Hayes · Score 92 — *"Viewed 650 Maple × 4 today"* → [Approve] [Why?]
  - **Card 2 ⏰ DEADLINE** — Johnson Closing · 72 hrs — *"Inspection still open, 2 tasks overdue"* → [Approve] [Why?]
  - **Card 3 ⚡ PAUSED** — Bloom Outreach · Paused — *"2 leads bounced — auto-paused"* → [Approve] [Why?]
- Each card has a tiny footer: `Powered by Lofty Lead Analysis` / `Powered by Transaction Checklists` / `Powered by Smart Plans` — shows we ride on existing Lofty features, not replace them.
- Voice closes: *"Three moves. Say go and I'll handle them, or approve the ones you want."*
- Bottom-right escape hatch: *"Explore the full Lofty dashboard →"* (small, 40% opacity).

### Screen 5 — THE EXECUTION (6s)
Agent approves all three (demo auto-approves). Each card collapses to a green ✓ row. Orb flashes cyan + chimes on each. Voice: *"Done. I'll check back in an hour. Anything else?"* Input field below: *"Ask anything…"*.

### Screen 6 — THE WHY (optional deep-dive)
Tapping **Why?** opens the Lead Detail (Jacob's existing screen, restyled). Shows score breakdown: Listing Activity +35, Email Engagement +28, Return Visit +20, Contact Quality +9. AI-drafted text ready to Edit or Send. Small callout: *"Previously Lofty showed a number. Now you see why — and act."*

### Screen 7 — PITCH SLIDE (submission form / static)
Before vs After table, 5 rows. Success metrics. Built at GlobeHack.

## Voice personality guide
- **Tone:** warm, competent, brief. No filler. No "as an AI…"
- **Pace:** human, with breaths between beats. Never rushed.
- **Vocabulary:** real estate fluent — "closing," "inspection," "bounce," "Smart Plan," "pipeline." No generic CRM jargon.
- **Confidence calibration:** when unsure, says so. *"I'm not confident enough to act on this one — want to review together?"*

## Orb state grammar
- **Idle:** slow 2-second breathe, soft glow
- **Thinking:** faster pulse, rainbow edge shimmer
- **Speaking:** concentric ripples outward every 1s
- **Executing:** bright cyan flash + inward-drawing rings
- **Done:** steady soft glow

## Sound cues (subtle, ≤0.3s)
- Orb appears: soft rising chime
- Each ✓ approved: tiny bell ping
- All done: soft descending resolution tone
- Error: muted low tone (rare)
